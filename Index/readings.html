Reading Response 2: the Poetry of Tools

In the opening paragraph of her post, Seu asks you, the reader to join her to 'defamiliarize our tools.' What do you think she means by that? And how do you think that action relates to her use of the word poetry?

In “The Poetry of Tools” article, Mindy Seu describes the relationship the reader has with their technology while reading an article. She describes an experience that majority of the people have on a day to day basis with their computer or any other tech supporting human interaction. She points out to the tools like type, click, send, swipe, and introduces her argument of reconstructing them. She wants to make the experience feel more natural, and less abnormal, as it become now. It has to relate back to the natural world use, that we experience everyday with other objects, and need to create that in the digital atmosphere. She uses word poetry to describe it, and I think its very good metaphor. When we hear a good written poetry, it goes with a flow, start doesn’t feel like start and end doesn’t feel like the end. Something very different from current experience that feels more task oriented.
